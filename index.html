Frequently Asked Questions

Good questions deserve
straight answers

Is this legal advice?
No. This binder is a professional HR template designed for coffee shop operations. It provides general guidance and best practices based on federal and state law, but it is not a substitute for advice from a licensed employment attorney. We always recommend having an attorney review your final policies, especially for state-specific requirements.
What does "customized" actually mean?
We replace all placeholder text with your actual business name, logo, and branding. We also tailor your state-specific wage rates, leave laws, and tip policies. You'll receive a binder that looks and reads like it was written specifically for your café — because it was.
Can I use the digital download for multiple locations?
The digital download is licensed for a single business location. For multiple locations, we strongly recommend our customized or premium packages — and we offer significant discounts (up to 50% off) for each additional location binder.
How long does customization take?
Typically 3–5 business days from your deposit, depending on how quickly you provide your business details. Once you approve the final version, your binder ships within 24 hours.
How does the policy update work in the Premium package?
Premium package owners receive one complimentary policy update, valid anytime within 5 years of purchase. If your state's minimum wage, leave laws, or other key employment regulations change, just reach out and we'll update your binder. You choose: a reprinted and shipped binder ($47) or a digital-only PDF update ($24). After your complimentary update is used, you can request additional updates anytime for the same low prices — for as long as you own the business.
Can Customized Binder owners get policy updates too?
Absolutely. Customized Binder owners can request a policy update at any time for $47 (reprinted + shipped) or $24 (digital PDF only). It's a simple, affordable way to keep your binder current without buying a whole new one.
