Questions

Things shop owners ask.

What POS systems do you work with?+
We work with Square, Toast, Lightspeed, Clover, and Revel. If you're on a different modern system, reach out before signing up and we'll confirm compatibility.
How do I share my data each month?+
Once signed up, we'll send you a simple guide for exporting your monthly reports from your POS. You email us the files and we take it from there — about 5 minutes on your end.
When will I receive my first report?+
Your first report covers your first full month after signup, delivered within the first week of the following month. Sign up in April, first report covers April, arrives early May.
What's the April promo pricing exactly?+
New subscribers who sign up in April pay $99 upfront — that covers your first 3 months at just $33/month. After month 3, the subscription transitions to $49.95/month. You'll receive advance notice before the price changes and can cancel anytime.
Can I book a strategy call without a subscription?+
Strategy calls are an exclusive benefit for subscribers, offered at a discounted rate and designed to discuss specific findings within your monthly report. For broader consulting needs, I do offer standard consulting services — for options and pricing please visit our Services page.
What if I have multiple locations?+
Multi-location data is incredibly valuable — the comparisons across locations reveal patterns you can't see otherwise. Reach out before signing up and we'll discuss a multi-location rate.
