Standard Monthly Subscription — $19.99/month

The standard subscription is billed monthly at $19.99. You may cancel at any time and your subscription will not renew after the current billing period ends.

No refunds are issued for partial months. If you cancel mid-month, you will continue to receive service through the end of your current billing period.

How the Service Works

Each month you will receive a custom PDF analytics report based on data exported from your POS system. You are responsible for exporting and submitting your monthly POS data by the agreed deadline. Reports are delivered within 7 business days of receiving your data.

This service requires a compatible modern POS system (Square, Toast, Lightspeed, Clover, or Revel). Bean & Ledger reserves the right to pause service if data is not submitted within 14 days of the monthly deadline.

General Terms

Your data is used solely for the purpose of producing your monthly report and is never shared with third parties. Bean & Ledger analytics reports are intended as business guidance tools and do not constitute financial, legal, or accounting advice.

Questions

Things shop owners ask.

What POS systems do you work with?+
We work with Square, Toast, Lightspeed, Clover, and Revel. If you're on a different modern system, reach out before signing up and we'll confirm compatibility.
How do I share my data each month?+
Once signed up, we'll send you a simple guide for exporting your monthly reports from your POS. You email us the files and we take it from there — about 5 minutes on your end.
When will I receive my first report?+
Your first report covers your first full month after signup, delivered within the first week of the following month. Sign up in May, first report covers May, arrives early June.
What's the May offer pricing exactly?+
New subscribers who sign up in May pay $45.99 upfront — that covers your first 3 months. After month 3, the subscription transitions to $19.99/month. You'll receive advance notice before the price changes and can cancel anytime.
Can I book a strategy call without a subscription?+
Strategy calls are an exclusive benefit for subscribers, designed to discuss specific findings within your monthly report. For broader consulting needs, visit our Services page.
What if I have multiple locations?+
Multi-location data is incredibly valuable — the comparisons across locations reveal patterns you can't see otherwise. Reach out before signing up and we'll discuss a multi-location rate.
