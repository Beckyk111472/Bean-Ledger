# ☕ Bean & Ledger: The Coffee Shop HR Binder

## 📌 Project Overview
Bean & Ledger provides specialized consulting for coffee shops and cafés. This repository hosts the landing page for our flagship product: **The Coffee Shop HR Binder**. 

Running a café is about more than just great coffee; it’s about people. We built this binder to solve the "real-world" headache of HR compliance, onboarding, and employee management that many owners struggle with.

## 🛠 Features of the Binder
* **50-State Compliance:** Wage reference tables and policy guidance for every state.
* **Onboarding Systems:** Checklists and forms from day one through the first week.
* **Industry Specific:** Built specifically for the flow of a café (tip pools, dress codes, and food safety).
* **Multiple Tiers:** Options ranging from instant digital downloads to fully customized, printed binders shipped to the shop's door.

We approach our work with the heart of a worker and the mind of an expert. Our goal is to humbly come alongside shop owners, investors, and partners to solve a systemic problem in the service industry: a lack of accessible, professional HR infrastructure.
